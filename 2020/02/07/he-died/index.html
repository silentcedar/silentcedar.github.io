He Died.

I don’t think he is a hero. When he made his choice, it was only out of good intentions, not so tragic as people said. Observing professional ethics and doing the right thing should not make him a hero.

But he must be a good man, at least he is morally better than me. Thinking in other words, after experiencing unfair treatment, I would definitely choose to protect myself and my family first(in fact, this is exactly what I’m doing now), instead of going back to work.

He is just a normal person like us, and his death makes me deeply realize that this kind of disaster may happen to each of us.

He deserves better.

We deserve better.

R.I.P
